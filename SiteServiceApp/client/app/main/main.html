SiteService presents a view of web service dependencies of selected websites.

You are able to see what services are most popular across the market, which sites are using those services and what sites use specific services

For example you are able to see that Google Analytics is used by a large percentage of web sites.

You can also see that Trademe.co.nz and Xero.co.nz are two sites that use Google Analytics.

In addition to the static information presented here, you may subscribe to receive email notifications of sites that begin to use new services.

You will then have visibility of new services being used in the market.
